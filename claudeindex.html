About me

I'm a software engineer focused on graphics programming and real-time systems. I completed my degree at DigiPen Institute of Technology Europe-Bilbao, where I specialized in applying mathematics, physics, and C++ to build renderers, game engines, and simulations from scratch.

I enjoy working close to the metal — whether that's building a custom memory manager, implementing pathfinding algorithms, or writing shaders for global illumination systems.
